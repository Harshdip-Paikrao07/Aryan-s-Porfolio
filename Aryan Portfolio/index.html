AboutMe

Hook-Based Short-Form Video Editor

I’m a short-form video editor who turns raw clips into scroll-stopping, high-retention Shorts and Reels. I specialize in creating hooky, fast-paced edits that grab attention in the first 3 seconds and keep viewers watching till the end. I previously ran and edited content for my own YouTube channel with 1.9M+ subscribers, where I learned firsthand what drives hooks, pacing, captions, and audience retention at scale. This experience shaped my editing style around what actually works on today’s platforms — YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels. From clean cuts to viral captions, I edit with one goal in mind: make people stop scrolling and start watching.
